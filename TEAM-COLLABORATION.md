## 🔧 HOW TO USE

### Configure Your Team

1. **Open Sidebar** → Look for "🤝 AI Team" panel
2. **Click ⚙️ Team Config**
3. **Select 2-5 Models** from available specialists
4. **Choose Team Dynamics**:
   - 🎯 Consensus (default, recommended)
   - 🎓 Specialist Mode
   - 💬 Debate Mode
   - 🧬 Synthesis Mode
5. **Save Configuration**

### Get Team Consensus

1. **Type Your Question**
2. **Click "🎯 Get Team Consensus"**
3. **Watch** team discuss in real-time
4. **See** each model's perspective
5. **Read** integrated team consensus
